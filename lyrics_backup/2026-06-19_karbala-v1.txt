[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
Deen-e-Haq ki aabroo hai arz-e-Karobala
La Ilaha ki baqaa hai arz-e-Karobala
Har ghareeb ka sahara arz-e-Karobala
Arsh ka Haseen nazara arz-e-Karobala

[Verse 1]
Aik basti ajab dasht mein hai saji
Jis ki mitti bani har maraz ki dava
Ishq-e-Rab ki haqeeqat vahan khul gayi
Sar kata kar jahan muskraya jari
Pyaas ka maarka aisa barpa hua
Khoon-e-Saadaat se baagh-e-deen mil gaya

[Pre-Chorus]
Har nazar tishna lab aasmaan ki taraf
Mauj-e-darya larazti hai khauf-o-sharaf
Asar ka vaqt hai iltija ka samaan
Uth raha hai zameen se ajab ik dhuan

[Chorus]
Sultanat ishq ki Ya Hussain Ya Hussain
Dil ka eemaan meri jaan Hussain Ya Hussain
Shah-e-Karobala Badshah-e-Huda
Tera parcham rahe ta qiyamat khula
Ya Hussain Ya Hussain Ya Hussain Ya Hussain
Ya Hussain Ya Hussain Ya Hussain Ya Hussain

[Crowd Response]
Labbaik Ya Hussain! Labbaik Ya Hussain!
Labbaik Ya Hussain! Labbaik Ya Hussain!

[Verse 2]
Vo Alamdar-e-Ghazi jari bawafa
Mashk thaame hue Alqama par gaya
Baazu kat bhi gaye par alam na jhuka
Haq-e-saqqai dasht-e-bala mein diya
Naam-e-Ghazi jahan bhi pukara gaya
Har mawali ka qismat ka taara jaga

[Pre-Chorus 2]
Teer chalte rahe azm-e-Shabbir par
Sajda-e-aakhri halq par khanjar tale
Aik paighaam goonja falak par ayaan
Bach gaya La Ilaha zair-e-tegh-e-sitam

[Chorus]
Sultanat ishq ki Ya Hussain Ya Hussain

Shah-e-Karobala Badshah-e-Huda
Tera parcham rahe ta qiyamat khula
Ya Hussain Ya Hussain Ya Hussain Ya Hussain
Ya Hussain Ya Hussain Ya Hussain Ya Hussain

[Crowd Response 2]
Maula Hussain Maula! Maula Hussain Maula!
Maula Hussain Maula! Maula Hussain Maula!

[Bridge]
Madrasa ban gayi dasht ki yeh zameen
Dars-e-hurriyat-e-Mustafa de gayi
Zainab-e-Paak ne Shaam ke qasr mein
Khutbon se zaalimon ki hukoomat hila di
Haar kar bhi Hussaini yahan jeet gaye
Naam baatil ka duniya se mit kar raha

[Spoken]
Haq hamesha rahe ga salamat yahan
Karbala hai gawah-e-urooj-e-fughaan

[Final Chorus]
Sultanat ishq ki Ya Hussain Ya Hussain
Dil ka eemaan meri jaan Hussain Ya Hussain
Shah-e-Karobala Badshah-e-Huda
Tera parcham rahe ta qiyamat khula
Saeed is dar ka adna sa sogwaar hai
Maatam-e-Shah mein ashkbaar-e-wafa rahe
Ya Hussain Ya Hussain Ya Hussain Ya Hussain
Ya Hussain Ya Hussain Ya Hussain Ya Hussain

[Outro]
Zindabad Karbala! Zindabad Ya Hussain!
Tajdar-e-Iram! Shahriyar-e-Zaman!
Ya Hussain Ya Hussain Ya Hussain Ya Hussain
Deen panah-e-jahaan Ya Hussain Ya Hussain
